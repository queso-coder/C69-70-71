Review On The Movies

Avatar: The Way Of The Water
Decent movie to watch. Max 3 times to watch and then you mostly like will get a bit bored.

I watched with my family in 3D and it didn't enhance much to the movie. A great bonding and enlighting movie although the story moves slow. You don't really have to watch the first movie first to get an understanding of the movie but you have to familiarize yourselves with the characters to be able to follow. If you want to watch it, I would wait until it comes out onto streaming platforms lilke Netflix or Hulu. One thing that was oustanding compared to the other details in the movie is the graphics. It was super realistic at the same time a bit too much for 3D.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse
A wonderful movie that always keeps you on your toes and guessing

I watched this movie first in the theaters with my sister and we both loved it. It had a very intersting storyline and is fit for all ages to watch which is a hard thing to accomplish especially since is in a comic style animation movie. The background music is very fitting and enhances the movies qualities. You have to watch the first movie to know the charcaters and to be able to follows, espcecially since it is a fastpaced movie. This movie is also very humorous and full of comedy. There is a good balnce betwee all aspects of this movie. Would highly reccomend for family movie night.
